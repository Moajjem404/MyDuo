### 🌟 Key Highlights

- **🔒 100% Serverless Privacy**: No central database or third-party servers storing your data.
- **🤖 Powered by Telegram**: Relies directly on your personal Telegram Bot as the end-to-end transport channel.
- **⚡ Real-Time App Sync**: Instantly view what application your partner is using with dynamic status indicators.
- **📜 Last 10 Activity History**: Tracks recent app updates with an automated 10-item storage cap to keep data minimal.
- **📡 Offline/Online Auto Sync**: Detects network connection loss and automatically catches up missed updates when back online.
- **💖 Premium Glassmorphic UI**: Beautiful dark romantic aesthetic with interactive micro-animations.

---

### 🧠 How It Works

```
[ Your Phone ]  <--->  [ Your Private Telegram Bot ]  <--->  [ Partner's Phone ]
```

1. **Direct Communication**: State updates (e.g. YouTube, Chrome, Screen Lock) are encrypted locally on your device.
2. **Personal Transport**: The app communicates strictly with **your own Telegram Bot** via standard HTTPS requests.
3. **Zero Third-Party Storage**: Your activity history and credentials stay **only on your phone** and inside your private Telegram chat group.

---

### 🚀 Quick Setup

1. Create a Telegram Bot via [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather) and get your **Bot Token**.
2. Create a private Telegram Group with your partner, add your Bot, and get the **Group ID**.
3. Open **MyDuo**, enter the Bot Token and Group ID, and you are ready to stay connected!
